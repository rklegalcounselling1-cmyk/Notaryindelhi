What Are Urgent Notary Services?

Urgent notary services refer to quick notarization of legal documents when they need to be submitted immediately to courts, banks, government departments, or other authorities.

A Notary Public verifies the identity of the person signing the document and confirms that the document is signed voluntarily. After verification, the notary affixes an official notary stamp and signature, making the document legally valid.
