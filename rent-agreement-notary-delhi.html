What is a Rent Agreement?

A Rent Agreement is a legal contract signed between a landlord (property owner) and a tenant (person renting the property). The agreement specifies important details such as:

  • Rent amount
  • Security deposit
  • Duration of tenancy
  • Property address
  • Rights and responsibilities of landlord and tenant

To make the agreement more reliable and legally recognized, the document is often signed before a Notary Public and notarized.

A notarized rent agreement confirms that both parties signed the document voluntarily and that their identities were verified.
